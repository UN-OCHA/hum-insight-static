World Health Organization
Health

The Health Sector is a platform for organizations to work in partnership to ensure collective action results in more timely, effective and predictable response to humanitarian health needs in north-east Nigeria. The sector gathers and disseminates sound and relevant information to guide partners’ response and identify and address gaps in technical knowledge to ensure the health response follows global best practices and standards.

Sector Objectives

  • CO1

    To provide life-saving and life-sustaining humanitarian health assistance to affected people.

  • CO3

    To strengthen the Health sector’s coordination and information systems to improve life-saving health response for people in need, with an emphasis on enhancing protection and increasing access to health services.

  • CO2

    To expand and strengthen the communicable disease surveillance for prevention/mitigation and control of epidemic outbreaks.
